MyTake uses Chrome's built-in Gemini Nano AI to reimagine web content instantly — entirely offline, entirely yours.

A guide · Three considered steps
The Method
IStep One

Enable the Prompt API

Navigate to the following address in your Chrome browser:

chrome://flags/#prompt-api-for-gemini-nano

Set the dropdown menu to Enabled.

IIStep Two

Optimization Guide

Then, visit this second address:

chrome://flags/#optimization-guide-on-device-model

Select Enabled BypassPerfRequirement from the menu.

IIIStep Three

Relaunch Chrome

Click the blue Relaunch button at the bottom-right of the flags page. Once Chrome restarts, MyTake will be ready to accompany you across the open web.
